Welcome

Hello! I'm a student with a deep passion for Economics, Law, Finance, and Philosophy. My academic journey is driven by an interest in understanding the complex interconnections between these fields, from the intricate workings of global markets to the ethical and philosophical foundations that shape legal systems and economic theories. I'm particularly fascinated by how economic principles influence legal frameworks and vice versa, and how finance plays a critical role in shaping both the corporate world and public policy. My studies push me to explore the balance between practical financial strategies and philosophical considerations of justice, equity, and the role of institutions. This website is a reflection of my intellectual pursuits and a platform to share my thoughts, projects, and future endeavors.
